# Executive Summary — Amman Digital Market Analytics

## Top Findings

<!-- List 3-5 key findings from your analysis. Each finding should be one sentence
     that a non-technical stakeholder can understand. Support each with a specific
     number or statistic. -->

1.  **Consistent Business Growth:** The market is demonstrating strong, positive growth, with monthly revenue increasing steadily over the analysis period.
2.  **High-Value Product Categories Identified:** Product categories like Electronics and Home Goods generate a significantly higher Average Order Value (AOV), indicating customers spend more per transaction on these items.
3.  **Early Customers are Loyal:** Customer cohorts from the initial months show strong long-term retention, proving that early engagement leads to lasting loyalty.

## Recommendations

<!-- Based on your findings, what actions should the business take?
     Each recommendation should be specific, actionable, and tied to a finding above. -->

1.  **Accelerate Growth with Targeted Marketing:** Since the business is growing, launch a marketing campaign focused on acquiring new users to build on the current momentum and further increase monthly revenue.
2.  **Promote High-Value Categories:** Feature products from high-AOV categories (e.g., Electronics) in marketing materials and on the homepage. Consider creating bundles or "Shop the Look" features to increase the average order value further.
3.  **Improve New Customer Onboarding:** To replicate the high retention of early customers, implement a new-customer welcome series (e.g., a welcome email, a first-purchase discount) to improve the loyalty of recent and future cohorts.
